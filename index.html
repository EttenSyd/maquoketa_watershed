This website hosts a dashboard to communicate water quality results from the Maquoketa Watershed.

Move your mouse over the map to zoom in or out and see different areas. You can click on points or locations to learn more. Use the filters or buttons around the map to change what information is displayed. Click the home button on the map to recenter the results.

Data collected and provided by the Maquoketa Watershed team.
Last updated September 2025.
